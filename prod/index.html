Rules
and Conditions

  • Who takes part in the contest?

    All the affiliates of the AdCombo affiliate network are the participants.

  • What offers should I manage traffic to?

    You should manage your traffic to all offers of AdCombo, CTR.ru, and Ecombo Junior for all GEOs.

    NB: there are special rates for some offers. For instance, redirect offers have a reduction rate of x0.25. Contact your AM for more information.

  • How does it work?

    A new section in the user interface will appear. You’ll be able to check the current quantity of Porsche Points you gained and your actual position in the Top-ranking table (Top-10, Top-20 , Top-30, Top-40, Top-50 or under Top-50).

    The more Porsche Points you get, the higher your position is.

  • What are the “Porsche Points” and how can I get them?

    Porsche Points are the contest currency. You get 1 Porsche Point per each 10 000 USD earned and available for withdrawal in your affiliate account. There are special rates for the conversion of funds into Porsche Points for some types of offers: (funds earned) x (offer rate).

    Example: an affiliate has run traffic in the period from Feb 1, 2020, to Sep 1, 2020, inclusive and earned 350 000 USD available for withdrawal. Hence, he got 35 Porsche Points.

    ATTENTION: An affiliate cannot get any Porsche Points for unapproved or rejected leads as well as for the held funds.

  • What changes will the AdCombo Loyalty Program face?

    The sum gained with the Loyalty Program will be on hold and not be available for withdrawal, but it will be displayed on the affiliate's dashboard and in the chart.

    The maximum percentage of the affiliate's income from the AdCombo Loyalty Program cannot be higher than 4%.

  • Who can participate in the prize drawing?

    The affiliates entered TOP-50 by the end of the contest are eligible to take part in the prize draw at the party.

    NB: Only those who are present at the final party can have a right to be full participants in the prize drawing.

    The date and time of the party will be announced later. Don't miss the updates!

    ATTENTION: AdCombo undertakes to provide the participants from the Top-50 with free accommodation at the venue of the final party at its expense. All travel expenses are paid by the participants themselves.

  • How many prizes can a prize draw participant win?

    Each participant of the prize drawing is able to win 1 additional prize and 1 grand prize (one of the two Porsche sports cars).

  • What is the procedure of choosing a winner?

    The winner is chosen by a draw.

    Each of the prize draw participants receives chips with its nickname registered in the AdCombo network. A quantity of chips of a participant is equal to the number of gained Porsche Points.

    The chips of all the prize draw participants are put into a lottery drum. The drawing for the additional prizes is conducted first. Then the drawing of the grand prizes starts.

Follow our news and updates and send a ticket request to your personal manager or contact AdCombo Support if you have a question to ask.

The AdCombo affiliate network reserves the right to impose restrictions toward incoming traffic or suspend it temporarily due to the technical need of the traffic processing system. Moreover, AdCombo is able to set caps on offers or place a limit on the number of leads.
